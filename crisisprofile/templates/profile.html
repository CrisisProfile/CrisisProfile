CrisisProfile No matter how vulnerable, decompensated, or uncooperative a person in crisis is CrisisProfile exist to ensure stakeholders never give up on anyone and put in 100% coordinated professional effort to prevent and de-esclate any crisis without harm

Think of CrisisProfile as Facebook for people and animals in crisis or likely to be in crisis. It is designed for life and death crises and applicable to any crisis such as those in business. It doesn't matter that you are struggling to apply a 300 page book to your life and work because CrisisProfile gives you just in time information in bite size pieces.
